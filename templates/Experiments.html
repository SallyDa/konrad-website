Model Output

For each experiment, there is a choice of model output. The temperature is the new equilibrium temperature of the atmosphere, and it sometimes takes thousands of model years to reach this state. There is also the option to look at the longwave and shortwave radiative heating rates, as well as the convective heating rate. In equilibrium, the three heating rates should balance.
